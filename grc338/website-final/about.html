About Us

At SUA Tea, we're more than just a boba shop; we're a haven for tea enthusiasts and bubble tea lovers alike. Nestled in the heart of our vibrant community, SUA Tea offers an escape into a world of exquisite flavors, where every sip is a blend of tradition, quality, and innovation.

Our teas are sourced from the finest estates, ensuring each cup starts with a base of unparalleled quality. We pride ourselves on our wide selection that ranges from timeless classics to modern twists, including signature milk teas, refreshing fruit teas, and artisanal blends unique to SUA Tea.

SUA Tea is more than just a place to grab a drink—it's a community. We host events, workshops, and meet-ups that celebrate the art of tea making, culture, and connection. Our cozy, welcoming space is designed to be your go-to spot for relaxation, study, and meeting friends old and new.

At SUA Tea, every cup tells a story, and we can't wait to share ours with you. Whether you're a boba aficionado or new to the world of tea, we invite you to discover your favorite blend, make memories, and experience the warmth and hospitality that is SUA Tea.
